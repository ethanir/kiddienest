## Domain checking notes

Before choosing a final name, check:

- .com domain
- .app domain
- .io domain
- GitHub organization availability
- Instagram/TikTok/LinkedIn handle
- Apple App Store search
- Google search
- USPTO trademark search

Do not buy a domain until the name is checked for conflicts.
